Give your pet the ultimate spa treatment.  The Fritzy's Pet Care Pros are gentle yet thorough.  We practice the lowest stress form of grooming for your pet and provide the most convenient service for you.

To start the Fritzy Fresh Spa Treatment we carefully clean your pet's eyes and ears, clip their nails and brush out their coat.  Then it's time for the warm water hydrotub bath.  Using all natural shampoos, we scrub and massage, ensuring that every part of your pet has two full minutes of gentle, deep soapy saturation.  The warm soothing rinse is repeated to make sure that the skin is soap free and ready to dry.  We hand dry every pet and we NEVER use cages!  We start with a good towel dry and then finish with a full brush out, some quick snips of the scissors to clean things up and a delicious treat for your pet.

But we're not done yet.  We document  everything we've done or observed, giving you a complete understanding of your pet's condition including any recommendations for a follow-up with your vet. Finally, we leave you with written information about our pet's service, health and helpful ideas for you and your pet.
