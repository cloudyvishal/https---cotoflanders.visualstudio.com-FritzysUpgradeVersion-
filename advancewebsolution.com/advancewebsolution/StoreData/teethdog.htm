How long has it been since you brushed your pet's teeth?  Brushing your pet's teeth is essential to a long healthy life.  Many studies have shown that your pet will keep its teeth later in life, have less infections, and be healthier overall with regular teeth brushing.  We use disposable tooth brushes (unless your pet has its own) to guarantee that there is no cross contamination between pets.  We angle the toothbrush at a 45 degree optimal position to stimulate the gums and remove the maximum amount of pre-tartar buildup.  Your pet's teeth will be sparkling clean and your dog's breath, Fritzy Fresh!
